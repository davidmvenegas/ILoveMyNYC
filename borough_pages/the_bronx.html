The Bronx

The Bronx is the only borough located in the mainland United States - all the others are islands. Among other things, the Bronx is the birthplace of hip-hop, jazz and where the New York Yankees became a dynasty. It is also home to lots of nature. Many come to see hundreds of different kinds of animals at the Bronx Zoo as well as to witness the spectacular flora of the New York Botanical Garden. The Bronx boasts its fair share of dining options from authentic Italian cuisine at Little Italy to mouthwatering seafood at the City Island Lobster House. Brimming with music, art and culture, one can simply walk any neighborhood to encounter incredible creative art works and hidden gems lining the street. There’s no other borough with a closer-knit community and rich cultural experiences.
