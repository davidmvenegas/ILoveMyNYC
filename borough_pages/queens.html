Queens

Queens covers nearly 40 percent of NYC's total area. Its vast space allows it to be home to the most active industrial area in the city and two of the largest international airports on the east coast. Queens is the most ethnically diverse urban area in the world, with nearly half of all Queens residents being foreign-born. Residents often closely identify with their neighborhood rather than with the borough or city, which leads to a number of exciting community events such as Greek Festivals, Italian fairs, and more. In the athletic arena, the New York Mets hail from Queens, where they play at Citi Field in Flushing Park, the same place the US Open is held each year. The remarkable mix of cultures, amazing food and family-friendly neighborhoods make Queens a perfect place for everyone.
