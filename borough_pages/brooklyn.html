Brooklyn

Brooklyn is New York City’s most populous borough with nearly 40 percent of the City’s total population, and continuing to grow. This borough is home to a diverse and growing retail market, entertainment, and residential areas. For hundreds of years, Brooklyn has maintained its ethnic communities which all celebrate their culture with delicious eateries and shops. There are a multitude of outdoorsy activities from places like Prospect Park, where you can do everything from pedal boats to horseback riding, to Coney Island - the legendary amusement parks filled with rides, games, corn dogs, and more. At the end of the day, it’s all too easy to fall in love with Brooklyn’s expanding cultural scene and beautiful districts with tree-lined streets, all while surrounded in it’s ever-present vibrant atmosphere.
