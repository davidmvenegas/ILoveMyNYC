Citi Field

Citi Field

Citi Field is a baseball park and the home field of MLB’s New York Mets. Without a shadow of a doubt, Citi Field is the best baseball stadium in NYC - you won't find a bad seat in the house. There’s always plenty to do and experience when you visit the stadium.

Rockaway Beach

Rockaway Beach

The Rockaways are truly one of the best summer escapes in New York. With its relaxed, friendly vibe, Rockaway Beach feels a world away from NYC. And yet, this amazing place—where you can lounge on the beach, enjoy amazing food, grab a snack on the boardwalk, or take a sunset walk—is still part of NYC and just a subway ride away.

Unisphere

Unisphere

At 12 stories high and constructed with 700,000 pounds of stainless steel, the Unisphere is an icon of Queens and has the distinction of being the largest globe in the world.

Queensborough Bridge

Queensborough Bridge

A walk across the Queensboro Bridge—nearly three-quarters of a mile long—not only offers views of its striking geometric outline, but also a New York skyline with great views of Long Island City, the East River, and the Upper East Side of Manhattan.
