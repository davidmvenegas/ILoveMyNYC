Fort Wadsworth

Fort Wadsworth

Fort Wadsworth is the oldest military structure in the United States. During the American Revolution it was held by the British until the war was won, after which it was reinforced and used to defend New York until its closing in 1994.

FDR Boardwalk

FDR Boardwalk

The Franklin D. Roosevelt Boardwalk follows Staten Island's eastern shore providing a recreational area for sports, picnicking and bird watching. Highlights include spectacular views of the Verrazano Bridge, the Atlantic Ocean and South Beach.
