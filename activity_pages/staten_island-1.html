Snug Harbor

Snug Harbor Cultural Center

Snug Harbor Cultural Center & Botanical Garden, a distinguished Smithsonian Affiliate, is Staten Island's premier destination for culture and entertainment. Set within a stunning 83-acre park-like setting, Snug Harbor presents a unique blend of gardens, museums, theaters, and seasonal festivals.

Staten Island Zoo

Staten Island Zoo

The Staten Island Zoo showcases more than 1,200 animals nestled in a beautiful 8-acre park, reflecting more than 350 species of birds, reptiles, amphibians, fish and invertebrates. The zoo also maintains more than 70 endangered & threatened species and is exceptionally acclaimed for its Serpentarium, housing an extensive collection of rattlesnakes.

Cousins Paintball

Cousins Paintball is a state-of-the-art outdoor paintball park with 20 acres of woods, hills, streams, and bunkers. Enjoy a day filled with thrills and adventure as you and your team engage in games of "Hide and Seek", "Cops and Robbers", and "Capture The Flag".
