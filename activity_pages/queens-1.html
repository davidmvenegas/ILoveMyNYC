Jamaica Bay Riding

Jamaica Bay Riding

Take advantage of some of the most scenic horseback riding New York has to offer. Enjoy a picturesque and pleasurable ride through beautiful wooded trails with breathtaking views of nature and beaches.

Unarthadox

Unarthadox

Unarthodox is a creative space where you take part in unique, immersive and interactive experiences using various forms of art. Sculpt blindfolded, test your art skills in a word-guessing game show, or paint solely on your intuition. Each curated experience is guaranteed to trigger your senses and leave you feeling inspired.

The Cliffs

The Cliffs

Not only do The Cliffs have the largest indoor climbing gym in NYC, but also a fair sized regular gym, slackline area and yoga classes. Advance your skills and have fun, with climbing classes ranging all the way from beginner to advanced.

Bowlero

Bowlero

At Bowlero Queens, you can discover a brand-new way to party and play with 35 lanes of blacklight bowling, and a state-of-the-art arcade. Hit the lanes and bowl while enjoying life-size video walls playing concerts, live sports, and more.
