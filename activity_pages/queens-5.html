Astoria

Astoria

If you're looking for a place with an artistic edge and a spotlight on social life, Astoria is that place. There’s always an abundance of opportunities to stay active, eat well, and explore the neighborhood. You can’t beat Astoria Park’s view of the city, eclectic architecture, or the inclusive community.

Long Island City

Long Island City

Long Island CIty is an energetic yet approachable neighborhood to live, work, and play in. It’s well-balanced, 19th-century once-industrial charm is nestled along a classic NYC waterfront minutes from Manhattan. The clean-lined architecture and serene public spaces make it feel modern and sleek, yet still charmingly under the radar.

Sunnyside

Sunnyside

Sunnyside is an escape for families and young professionals with a thriving restaurant scene. The neighborhood's tree-lined streets, brick faced architecture, and the blend of urban and suburban homes offer plenty of family friendly hangouts - a haven for foodies and dog owners.

Jackson Heights

Jackson Heights

Jackson Heights is a dynamic neighborhood that thrives on its diverse identity. It’s food scene is unmatched, a buffet of international offerings giving you a world of cuisines at your fingertips. It’s global inclusivity and community vibe guarantees you’ll never run out of fresh discoveries.
