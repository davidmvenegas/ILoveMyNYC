The Rink

The Rink at Rockefeller Center

Ice skating on The Rink under the Rockefeller Center Christmas Tree is a classic New York winter experience for locals and tourists alike. From ice skating lessons to special events, The Rink sets the perfect scene for every occasion. It’s a tradition that has never failed to delight and you’re always invited to join in on the fun.

Spyscape

If you've ever fantasized about going on a secret spy mission, then look no further than Spyscape. Experience spy stories in an immersive environment, try an actual spy challenge, and test your espionage skills.

VR World

VR World

VR World is the largest Virtual Reality Entertainment Destination in North America, offering over 50 virtual reality experiences from single-player to multiplayer games to film, art and documentaries. Zero experience needed. Fight a pro-boxer, jam with your favorite artists, or live out a zombie apocalypse.

Escape the Room
