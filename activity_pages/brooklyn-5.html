Crown Heights

Crown Heights is a diverse area bursting with energy and things to do. Community oriented and unpretentious with afternoons passed relaxing and loads of destination-worthy dining. Housing is increasingly competitive, but there is always great value available.

Bushwick

Bushwick

Bushwick is an emerging cultural center of Brooklyn with both Michelin-starred restaurants and $3 street tacos. Bushwick holds all the best Brooklyn has to offer and has quickly become home to artists, musicians and all sorts of creative types.
