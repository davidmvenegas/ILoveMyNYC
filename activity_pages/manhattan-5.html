SoHo

SoHo

SoHo’s iconic and creative vibe is established by the neighborhood’s resident artists, quaint cafes, cast-iron structures and bohemian energy. Here you’ll have the pulse of downtown NYC at your fingertips with endless options to explore for shopping, dining, and entertainment.

Chelsea

Chelsea

Chelsea has great nightlife and an easy commute to anywhere in Manhattan. With numerous gallery openings, outdoor lounging, and a new place to discover around every corner, you’ll fall in love with Chelsea’s class and culture.

Hell's Kitchen

Hell's Kitchen

This is a neighborhood that has a little bit of everything: culture, entertainment, and convenience. With bustling streets, diverse restaurants and a welcoming atmosphere this up-and-coming neighborhood continues to evolve while staying close to its roots.

TriBeCa

TriBeCa

Here you can enjoy the entertainment perks of living in downtown NYC, but with a slightly quieter neighborhood to call home. Take peaceful strolls along the Hudson River late at night, and feel the elegance of Manhattan, tucked aside from it’s busy downtown.
