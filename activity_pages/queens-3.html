Color Factory

Color Factory

Color Factory is a collaboratively produced art experience in Queens. This multi-sensory exhibit, inspired by the colors of the City, guides visitors through 16 participatory installations, designed to awaken you to the everyday presence of color.

Museum of the Moving Image

Museum of the Moving Image

The Museum of the Moving Image is the only institution inAmerica dedicated to the social impact of film, television and digital media. Experience the creative process of making moving images through a unique combination of interactive experiences, one-of-a-kind artworks and demonstrations of professional crafts and equipment.

New York Hall Of Science

New York Hall Of Science

Visit the New York Hall of Science to discover more than 450 interactive, hands-on exhibits including Rocket Mini Golf, a massive Science Playground, Design Lab—an innovative hands-on space that allows visitors to gain a greater understanding of the design and engineering process—as well as much more.

Queens Museum

Queens Museum

The Queens Museum’s exhibitions and collections are second to none. Notably is their three-dimensional scale model over 90000 sq. ft. of New York and it’s five boroughs, and is regularly updated. The Museum is dedicated to the highest quality visual arts in Queens—a diverse, ethnic, cultural and international community.
