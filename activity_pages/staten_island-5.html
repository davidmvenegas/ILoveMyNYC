St. George

St. George

For its many daily commuters, St. George feels more like a peaceful extension of Downtown Manhattan. Within walking distance from the island’s 24/7 ferry system, the rest of the city is always just 25 minutes away. While the cost of living and noise pollution are low, NYC's electric vibe is still very much present.

Tompkinsville

Tompkinsville

Living in Tompkinsville offers an urban suburban mix feel. Streets wind around hills and backyards and gardens are commonplace. There are plenty of restaurants, coffee shops, and beautiful history. If you want to be close to the city action without being constantly surrounded by it, this fascinating and laid-back neighborhood is a perfect fit.

Randall Manor

Randall Manor

Randall Manor is the most charming and character laden neighborhood you will find in New York City. This neighborhood consists of mostly 1930's Tudor style homes alongside maples and oak trees that tower over the community streets and place them under a blanket of shade.

South Beach

South Beach

South Beach is one of Staten Island's most inviting neighborhoods. The shoreline provides sunbathing, swimming and fishing as well as parks that feature checkerboard tables, games and an always cheerful atmosphere. If you love the ocean and outdoor activities you’ll quickly fall in love with South Beach.
