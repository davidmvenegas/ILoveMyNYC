Prospect Park

Prospect Park

Prospect Park is a massive playground in the center of Brooklyn. Visitors can play softball, explore the Prospect Park Zoo, go horseback riding, stroll through the breathtaking Long Meadow, treat their four-legged friends to a dip at Dog Beach and ice-skate at the LeFrak Center.

Brooklyn Bridge Park

Brooklyn Bridge Park

Brooklyn Bridge Park, as its name would suggest, is stationed under the Brooklyn Bridge. Come for the awe-inspiring views of Manhattan and perfect picnic spots along the waterfront. The park has plenty of activities, including the free Movies With a View, kayaking, rowing and fitness classes, gardens, playgrounds, basketball courts, an in-line skating rink and a giant sandbox.

Marine Park

Marine Park

Brooklyn's largest park has something to offer for every outdoorsman. From bike paths, baseball fields, bocce ball, nature trails, and playgrounds to a 210-acre golf course and even a place to launch boats and kayaks in Jamaica Bay.
