High Rock Park

High Rock Park

As the cornerstone of Staten Island's massive Greenbelt, High Rock Park is known for its wetlands, hiking trails, plant life and wildlife. Visit this designated National Environmental Landmark for a tranquil and beautiful space to escape the bustling City and learn about nature.

Willowbrook Park

Willowbrook Park

Willowbrook Park is on the northern fringe of the Staten Island Greenbelt and is perfect for a family picnic or a stroll around the pond. Enjoy the Victorian-style merry-go-round, serene duck pond, and catch and release fishing. Willowbrook’s combination of natural wilderness offers a welcome contrast to the bustling neighborhood and streets that surround it.

Silver Lake Park

Silver Lake Park

Silver Lake Park is a 200+ acre oasis with facilities including sports fields, biking paths, dog runs and an 18-hole golf course. At the center of the park is the lake itself, which served as an ice harvesting and skating facility during the 19th century, and later was refashioned into a working reservoir.

Ocean Breeze Park

Ocean Breeze Park

Ocean Breeze Park has a beautiful topography of sand dunes, wetlands, grasslands, and a shrub forest. It’s a wonderful place to gather with friends, listen to music, and admire the greenery. Come kick back for a while and have fun.
