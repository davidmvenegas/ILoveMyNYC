Bronx Museum of the Arts

Bronx Museum of the Arts

The Bronx Museum of the Arts specializes in contemporary art from diverse cultural backgrounds, as well as art to which the Bronx itself plays a central role. Exhibitions and complementary public programs offer visitors the opportunity to learn about the Bronx, its heritage, history and culture while gaining insight into other cultural groups.

Universal Hip-Hop Museum

Universal Hip-Hop Museum

Anchored in the birthplace of the culture, the Universal Hip Hop Museum in the Bronx celebrates and preserves the history and culture of local and global Hip Hop to inspire, empower, and promote understanding.

City Island Nautical Museum

City Island Nautical Museum

This museum, which occupies a site that once served as Revolutionary War logistic grounds, offers visitors a taste of City Island's seafaring history. The museum's collection includes historical items like authentic ship sails, model boats, and photography related to the island's rich nautical past.

Bronx Documentary Center

Bronx Documentary Center

This Bronx Documentary Center is a nonprofit institution that hosts screenings, discussions and weekly meetings for local filmmakers. Through exhibitions, lectures and workshops the BDC shows the work of both internationally-renowned and emerging photographers and filmmakers.
